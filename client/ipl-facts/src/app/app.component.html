The Indian Premier League

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India contested teams representing the different cities in India. The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2008.

IPL is the most-attended cricket league in the world and in 2014 ranked sixth by average attendance among all sports leagues.
